The Lions Den Cigar Club


The purpose of The Lionâ€™s Den is to enhance the cigar experience of the member by providing hosting social functions and enjoying fellowship with other members.

1. The cigar is chosen by the host. A $15 max price is set for the cigars. If the host chooses a cigar that exceeds the $15 max, the host is responsible for the extra cost. Members will reimburse the host for the cigar at the time of the meeting.

2. Host will also choose which whiskey or cognac to pair the cigar with. The alcohol is not refunded

3. Host will choose time and location of meeting.

4. Membership of the Club will be by invitation only, with a max of 15 members. Anyone wanting to be a member will need to attend 3 consecutive meetings. After that time, original members will vote to see if membership invitation will be extended.

5. Members may invite friends; however, they will need to notify whomever is hosting in a timely manner

6. Members need to attend a minimum of 4 meetings in the given year.

7. If Member is not able to attend a meeting, the member is still responsible for picking up their cigar and filling out the tasting review.
